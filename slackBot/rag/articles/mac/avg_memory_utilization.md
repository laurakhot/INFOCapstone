# Case 1: 😵 100+ tabs (To be changed)

## Resolution Steps:
1. Close Unneeded Applications & Browser Tabs
2. Every open app and tab **consumes memory, even when idle**.
3. Make a habit of closing what you're not actively using, especially memory-hungry apps like Figma, Zoom, video editors, or virtual machines.

## What's happening
Your Mac is working harder than usual to keep everything running in memory.
When too many apps compete for RAM, your system can slow down, freeze, or crash unexpectedly.
