# Reducing Memory Usage on Your Windows

## Here's what to do

Let’s start with closing unneeded tabs and applications.

1. Press **Control + Shift + Esc**.
2. Click the application or background process that you want to force quit.
3. Click **End task**.

## What's happening
Your Windows is working harder than usual to keep everything running in memory.
When too many apps compete for RAM, your system can slow down, freeze, or crash unexpectedly.
