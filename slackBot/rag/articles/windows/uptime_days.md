# 😴 Sleep-Deprived Laptop

## Resolution Steps:
1. **Save any open work.** Restarting will close all apps.
2. **Open the Start menu.** Click the **Start** icon in the bottom-left corner or press the **Windows key**.
3. **Restart your PC.** Click the **Power** icon, then select **Restart**. Wait for it to fully restart — this may take a few minutes if updates are pending.
4. **Force restart if unresponsive.** Press and hold the **power button** for 10 seconds until the PC shuts off, wait a few seconds, then press it once to turn back on.

## What's happening
Your laptop has been running for a long time without a restart. Memory builds up, background processes pile on, and performance gradually degrades — leading to slowdowns, freezing, or unexpected crashes.

Closing the lid only puts your laptop to sleep, which preserves your session but doesn't clear memory or apply updates. Restarting once a week is a great habit: it clears memory, applies pending Windows updates, and keeps your laptop running at its best.
